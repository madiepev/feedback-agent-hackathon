Research Participation

Can AI Give Good Feedback? A Participatory Hackathon Study

Quick Summary: You're invited to participate in research exploring how students design AI feedback agents. Participation is completely voluntary and separate from the hackathon event itself.

Key Points

  • Voluntary: You can attend the hackathon without participating in the research
  • What's collected: Your summaries, agent designs, and feedback (all anonymized)
  • Duration: 3 hours during the hackathon event (June 17, 2026)
  • Privacy: GitHub usernames replaced with anonymous IDs (P001, P002, etc.)
  • Benefits: Contribute to improving AI feedback systems in education
  • Withdraw: You can withdraw at any time by emailing the researcher
How to Provide Consent:
By providing your GitHub handle in the consent form, you consent to participate in the research.

If you do not provide your GitHub handle, you do not consent and can still fully participate in the hackathon event.
